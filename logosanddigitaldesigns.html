LEAN

Developed a clean and minimalistic logo and brand colors for "Lean," a startup specializing in oxygen masks during the COVID-19 pandemic. The client specified a preference for black and blue as the primary colors to convey a sense of professionalism and trustworthiness. The design process involved creating a simple yet impactful logo that embodies the brand's commitment to providing essential health products with reliability and efficiency. The final design features a sleek and modern aesthetic, with a harmonious blend of black and blue, ensuring strong visual identity and brand recognition.
